The FNAF community has created hundreds of fangames since 2014. In 2020, Scott Cawthon launched the Fanverse Initiative, officially funding and supporting five fangame developers. This database catalogs every notable fangame from both the official Fanverse and the broader community, drawing from the FNAF Wiki and FNAF Fanon Wiki archives.

FANVERSE INITIATIVE (Officially Supported)

Announced August 2020 by Scott Cawthon. Provides funding, publishing, and merch support for selected fangame developers.

FNaC

Five Nights at Candy's (FNaC) — Emil Macko

Developer: Emil "Ace Macko" Macko. A trilogy of fangames set in Candy's Burgers & Fries. Features original animatronics: Candy the Cat, Cindy the Cat, Chester the Chimpanzee, and Vicky the Vixen. FNaC 1 (2015) follows guard Marylin Schmidt. FNaC 2 (2016) is a prequel with new animatronics (The Penguin, Blank, Old Candy). FNaC 3: The Final Chapter (2019) concludes with the burned "Ignited" animatronics. Known for its melancholic atmosphere, deep lore, and the twist that the protagonist is a ghost. Remake: FNaC: Reimagined (canceled). Part of Fanverse with planned remasters.

ONaF

One Night at Flumpty's (ONaF) — Jonochrome

Developer: Jonochrome. A surreal horror-comedy fangame featuring Flumpty Bumpty, an egg-shaped character with a face. ONaF 1 (2015): single night, instant jumpscares, characters include The Owl, The Red Man, Eyesaur. ONaF 2 (2016): expanded roster with Birthday Boy Blam, Golden Flumpty. One Week at Flumpty's (OWaF, 2017): seven-night finale, extreme difficulty modes, story conclusion. Known for its absurdist humor mixed with genuine horror. Jonochrome was the first Fanverse developer to release a completed remaster (ONaF 1 & 2 remastered). Development on OWaF remaster was halted after Jonochrome's controversy in 2021.

POPGOES

Popgoes Evergreen — Kane Carter

Developer: Kane Carter. Set in a fantasy world where animatronics are heroes. Popgoes (2016): gameplay centered on a security system with Blackjack, Saffron, Blake, and Stone. Popgoes Arcade (2017): prequel arcade-style game. Popgoes Evergreen (Fanverse reboot): complete reimagining with expanded lore, new characters, and modern graphics. Known for its colorful art style contrasting dark narrative, and for introducing the concept of "Weaponized Animatronics." Kane Carter is also known for creating the "Popgoes Pizzeria" series and contributing to the Freddit community.

TJoC

The Joy of Creation (TJoC) — Nikson

Developer: Tyler "Nikson" McLeod (BionicFlame). Unique fangame set in a house rather than a pizzeria. Freddy stalks the player through a family home. TJoC (2015): free-roam survival horror where Freddy can attack from any room. TJoC: Story Mode (2016): narrative-driven expansion. TJoC: Reborn (Fanverse): complete rebuild with Unreal Engine, enhanced mechanics, and expanded story. Known as one of the most terrifying fangames due to its free-roam nature and unpredictable AI. Nikson was also involved in the "FNaF World" fangame community.

FIVE NIGHTS AT WARIO'S SERIES

Notable fangame replacing animatronics with Nintendo characters in horror forms.

FNaW 1

Developer: Saveliy "Mister" Tkachenko. Reimagines Mario characters as nightmarish animatronics. Wario, Waluigi, and others stalk the player. Five Nights at Wario's (2015) launched the series. Known for its creative reinterpretation of Nintendo IP in the FNAF formula.

FNaW 2

Sequel expanding the roster with more Nintendo-inspired nightmares. New mechanics, cameras, and jump scares. Introduced Bowser, Yoshi as hostile animatronics.

FNaW 3

Third entry in the crossover series. Enhanced graphics, deeper mechanics, and a darker atmosphere. Concluded the original trilogy arc.

FIVE NIGHTS AT F***BOY'S SERIES

FNaFB 1

Developer: HylianDev / Sable. A visual novel/RPG hybrid combining FNAF characters with adult humor and turn-based combat. Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, Foxy, and others are reimagined as anthropomorphic female characters. Released 2015. Known for its comedic writing, parody elements, and surprisingly well-designed RPG mechanics.

FNaFB 2

Sequel expanding the cast with FNaF 2 characters (Toy animatronics, Puppet, Mangle). New combat system, more adult humor, and branching storylines. Characters: Toy Freddy, Toy Bonnie, Toy Chica, Mangle, Balloon Boy.

FNaFB 3

Final entry featuring FNaF 3 characters (Springtrap, Phantom animatronics). Deep narrative with multiple endings. Concluded the trilogy with a mix of comedy and genuine emotional beats.

OTHER NOTABLE FANGAMES

ADVENTURE

Five Nights at Treasure Island

Developer: Radiance. A fangame based on Disney's Treasure Island ride with original animatronics. Free-roam exploration. Known for its atmospheric horror and original character designs inspired by classic dark ride animatronics. Multiple versions released with evolving mechanics.

HORROR

Final Nights Series

Developer: V. Four-game series (Final Nights 1-4) set in new locations with original animatronics and lore. Known for its dark atmosphere, complex mechanics, and cinematic approach to storytelling. Each game features new security systems and environmental hazards.

COMEDY

Dayshift at Freddy's Series

Developer: Doggo. Comedy series where the player works the day shift. Dialogue-driven gameplay with pop culture references, humor, and a surprisingly deep narrative. Multiple entries including Dayshift at Freddy's 1, 2, 3, and 4. Features original characters and voice acting.

PLATFORMER

Super FNaF

A 2D platformer fangame combining FNAF characters with Mario-style gameplay. Players control Freddy through levels, collecting items and avoiding hazards. Multiple sequels expanded the concept with new characters and mechanics.

FANON

FNAF World: Different Universe

Fanon RPG expansion of FNAF World. New characters, worlds, and storylines created by the community. Features original animatronics, new abilities, and extended exploration beyond the original game. Documented on the FNAF Fanon Wiki.

SAGA

The Return Series

Fanon fangame series exploring the return of original animatronics. Multiple entries with interconnected storyline. New locations, animatronics, and gameplay mechanics. Chronicled extensively on the FNAF Fanon Wiki with 320+ articles of fanon content.

CROSSOVER

Five Nights at the Chum Bucket

Crossover between FNAF and SpongeBob SquarePants. Set in Plankton's Chum Bucket with SpongeBob characters as animatronics. Known for its humorous concept and creative enemy designs.

JOLLY

Jolly Series

Developer: J.M. Original fangame series with unique animatronics (Jolly, Ginger, Wooly, and others). Three main entries. Distinct art style and original lore separate from main FNAF canon.

SURVIVAL

Chaos at the Pizzaplex

Multiplayer survival fangame based on Security Breach's Pizzaplex setting. Co-op and competitive modes. Players survive against animatronics while completing objectives.

MANAGEMENT

Fazbear Tycoon

Pizzeria management simulation. Players build and manage their own Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Hire staff, purchase animatronics, handle incidents. Inspired by tycoon games and FNAF lore.

EXPLORATION

Fredbear's Fright

First-person exploration fangame set in Fredbear's Family Diner. Stealth-based gameplay with atmospheric horror. Players navigate the abandoned diner while avoiding threats.

ADDITIONAL FANON FANGAMES

From the FNAF Fanon Wiki — community-created game concepts and series.

BLOODY

Five Nights at Freddy's: Bloody Party

Fanon series concept. A birthday party gone wrong at Freddy's. Original animatronics and survival mechanics. Dark reinterpretation of the classic FNAF setup.

DISCARDED

AD: The Discarded Story

Fanon narrative about a forgotten animatronic left in storage. Psychological horror and mystery. Explores themes of abandonment and memory within the FNAF universe.

MYSTERY

FNAF: El misterio del local

Spanish-language fanon investigation series. A group of teens explore an abandoned restaurant. Original characters and location. Documented on the Spanish Fanon community.

REMAKE

FNAF: Stupid Saga

Fanon series of comedic shorts and games. Humorous situations with animatronics. Light-hearted take on the FNAF universe popular in Spanish-speaking community.

CONTROVERSIAL & DISCUSSED FANGAMES

TRFR

The Return to Freddy's Series

Developer: BFP. One of the most controversial fangame series. Expanded the lore with new restaurants and animatronics but faced legal action from Scott Cawthon for copyright infringement. Multiple versions released (1-5). The controversy led to important community discussions about fan game boundaries and intellectual property.

FNAF WORLD

FNAF World: Fanon Expanded Universe

Community expansion of the official FNAF World RPG. New characters, areas, and storylines created by fans. Includes original animatronics and crossover content from other fangames.
